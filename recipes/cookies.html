Sugar/Cinnamon Sugar Cookies

Description

Small batch, thin and chewy, sugar or cinnamon sugar cookies with crispy edges.

Yields 6 cookies

Ingredients

  • 76g (1/3 cup) salted butter
  • 80g (1/3 cup + 1 tbsp) sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 98g (3/4 cup + 1 tbsp) flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • Extra sugar and/or cinnamon for rolling

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350, and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. Combine softened or melted butter and sugar and beat until light and fluffy, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Add yolk and vanilla, and beat until combined.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt, and beat until dough comes together and starts to clump.
  5. Divide dough into 6 balls.
  6. Roll dough balls in sugar or cinnamon sugar and transfer them to the baking sheet.
  7. Use a flat bottomed glass to flatten each cookie to around 1/2" thick (for fluffier cookies skip this step).
  8. Bake for 9-12 minutes or until the bottom edges of the cookies are barely golden.
  9. Let cool still on sheet and enjoy!
