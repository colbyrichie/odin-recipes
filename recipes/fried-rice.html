Fried Rice

Description

Hibachi style fried rice to enjoy by itself or with added proteins such as chicken, shrimp, or steak.

This recipe is the culmination of many fried rice recipes experimented with over the years.

Ingredients

  • Rice (Preferably long grain rice such as jasmine or basmati)
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Eggs
  • Onion (White or Yellow) (Diced)
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Wash rice with cold water until the water runs clear
  2. Put rice in a pot and add water until it's about half a knuckles length above the rice
  3. Bring to a boil
  4. Once boiling turn burner to low, cover with lid, and let cook for 20 minutes
  5. After 20 minutes, move pot off of heat and let sit with lid on for 5-10 minutes
  6. While waiting for rice to steam off of heat, spread a little vegetable oil in a large pan, wok, or grittle
  7. Crack some eggs and quickly scramble them, then spread them in a thin sheet to cook
  8. Using a spatula, roll eggs up and set them to the side
  9. Lay rice down spread and move around in excess oil
  10. Chop eggs, and mix them in with the rice
  11. Move rice and eggs to the side
  12. Lay down onions with a little oil and cook until they start to become translucent, then add to the rice mixture
  13. Lay down garlic with oil, when fragrant, add to mixture
  14. Add a knob of butter to rice, and mix it in until melted and coating the rice
  15. Salt and pepper to taste
  16. Drizzle soy sauce, and alow to sit below rice for a few seconds before mixing in
  17. Mix until everything is well combined, serve and enjoy
